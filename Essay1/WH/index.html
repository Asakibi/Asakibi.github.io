Seipi: Lei

　　‘Y pushia ta mosi lei.’

　　Pu tsauy niau, lenshin iejieu uuei. Tuonienchien jiali ietshen tsaukuo leiji, kaisangti shieulijiang teu shieupute. Tan jinshie ɯtsi, putshen fasenkuo tsetsong ‘tshan-an’, iejieu maute mo kaipeishin, kai tshuei fongsan ti haisi tshuei tau. Tsitau jiajia tintau leishiang, sueimongtsong phachi, chiauchiau kuanliau ichie. Puchien takhai tshuanghu, iejiu pu liangkhuai.

　　Tseiangti tshangjin ijin jienkuo pushia sitshi. Meitshi jieuchiang tsuo uomong ipan, lautsi limien tsonghuei pha ishie puhauti tongshi chylai. Shiangtau hauieu tsenɯ meihuei uo shinshi pa, shiangtau kuo ituan sijien ieuiau thintienliau pa, tsongtsi shintsong tsueitsueipuan, lausi ieu tsatshau kenchy putiau, shinli nante chinjin. Tsitau tha hueifu, suo tha senpinliau, uo jinhai shyliau ikheu chi; kepi tenhuo hai liangtau, uo pien putsai tanshin mosi — io uo tsenti si tseme shiang, na uo jieusansi pankuo huotati shiauhuosang. Teiy tsensiti uo, puanti tongshi, itsi jieuchiangsi lieutongti iongien, putin mautau phau, shyshy chienshin. Ɯ uo ieu pushiang iong tsen tsapho tseshie iongiensang lelensen-ienti hongphau, in-uei thamen tshishiaupitsang, tshuenfongtshueiieusen.

　　Len pulen maute kualienti tongshi. Ieusiheu shiang, nashie tsitshautsuo meishinmeifeiti len, shinli tauti chiauchiau mailiau jituo tongshi, itsiy lien tsiji teu ufa tshajio?

　　Tan y tsongkuei haisi shia shialailiau. Minyntuotshuantau uchyluojioti lei, tsong haisi tipukuo la y ti.
